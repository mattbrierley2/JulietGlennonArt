Artist's Statement

Juliet Glennon’s work records experiences that accompany living with a non-normative state of mind. It explores what may be unknown to us, or what is beyond our shared field of awareness by documenting metaphysical experiences and insights. At times, her work is universal and communicates within a broader contextual narrative - it also serves as quiet conversation that addresses her past and present relationships through symbolism, theme, and memory.

About Juliet

Juliet was born and raised in the Los Angeles area, and now lives in the Pacific Northwest.

She has a Bachelor of Art in History (2019) focused on American Indigenous Histories, and has most credits earned toward a Master of Library and Information Science focused on Archiving.

She is an Equestrian, an Artist, a Poet, and a Survivor.
