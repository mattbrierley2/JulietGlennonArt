Artist — Juliet Glennon

Artist's Statement

My work exists as records of real experiences that accompany living with a non-normative state of mind. I explore what may be unknown to us, or what is beyond our shared field of awareness by documenting personal, mystic experiences and insights. At times, my work is universal and communicates within a broader contextual narrative - it also serves as quiet, intimate conversation addressing past and present relationships through symbolism, theme, and memory.

Bio

I was born and raised in the Los Angeles area, and now live in the Pacific Northwest.

BA in History (2019) focused on American Indigenous Histories, MLIS focused on Archiving.

Equestrian, Artist, Poet, Survivor.
